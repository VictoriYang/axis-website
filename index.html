关于 Axis

About Axis

"我们构建具有自主意识的具身智能"

"We build embodied intelligence with genuine autonomous will."

Axis 是一家专注具身智能的科技公司。我们相信，真正的智能需要一个身体来感知世界、理解世界、改变世界。

Axis is a technology company dedicated to embodied intelligence. We believe that true intelligence requires a body to perceive, understand, and transform the world.

我们的核心理念是赋予机器自主意志——不只是执行指令的工具，而是能够理解意图、做出判断、持续学习的智能体。

Our core philosophy is to endow machines with autonomous will—not just tools that execute commands, but intelligent agents capable of understanding intentions, making judgments, and learning continuously.
